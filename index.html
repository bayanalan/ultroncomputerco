About Me

My name is Maverick White and I am almost eleven years old. I really want to make powerful AR/VR tech to prove to Apple that the Vision Pro does not have to be fat and bulky. I am trying to build ar glasses that use spatial technology to open close and keep windows open. Please know that these computers are not open to the public and cannot be bought or demoed, but DIY tutorials will be posted on the Ultron Spatial Youtube Channel once complete. This is the new Ultron computers website, meaning that for a few weeks to months the domain name will still be ultroncomputer.net.

To donate, write a check and take a photo of it, then send it to us via the "Send Check" button. The check will be sent directly to us.

AR Glasses

These are glasses powered by the Radxa Zero 3W 8GB, which is a powerful computer the size of a Raspberry Pi Zero. The display is a 1080p VR Microdisplay capable of displaying items crisp and clear. All credit goes to Radxa for designing this tiny but powerful board.
